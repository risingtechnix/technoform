About Us

Technoform industries is a leading manufacturer company of vaccum forming-thermoforming parts . We have the HDPS/PP/HIPS/PET/ESD/CONDUCTIVE TRAY for manufacturing AS per customer requirements

TECHNOFORM INDUSTRIES PVT.LTD. The company has all the required legal registrations under the local laws.

The factory is located at a very modern industrial area developed by the state government about 50km from Pune. Its product range includes packaging and material handling solutions. The company also provides solutions in development and supply of engineering plastic components.
